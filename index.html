Popular Salary and Paycheck Questions

How much is $20 an hour per year?

That depends on how many hours you work each week and how many weeks you work per year. Our Hourly to Salary Calculator helps estimate annual income from hourly pay.

How do I convert annual salary to hourly pay?

You can divide annual salary by the number of weeks worked per year and then by hours worked per week. Our Salary to Hourly Calculator does this automatically.

How can I estimate take-home pay?

To estimate take-home pay, subtract taxes from gross income. Our Take-Home Pay Calculator gives a simple after-tax estimate based on the tax rate you enter.

How do I calculate weekly earnings?

Weekly earnings are usually calculated by multiplying hourly pay by hours worked per week. You can also estimate net weekly income after tax using our Weekly Pay Calculator.

Can I use these calculators for budgeting?

Yes. You can estimate income with our pay calculators and then use the Budget Calculator to plan expenses, savings, and spending percentages more clearly.

Are these calculators exact?

These tools provide simplified estimates and are helpful for planning, comparison, and quick calculations. Actual taxes, deductions, and pay policies may vary by job and location.
