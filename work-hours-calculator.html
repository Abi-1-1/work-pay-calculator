What Is a Work Hours Calculator?

A Work Hours Calculator helps you estimate how many total hours you worked over a set number of days and how much you may have earned from those hours. It is useful for employees, freelancers, part-time workers, and anyone who wants a simple way to track time and income.

If you know how many hours you work each day, how many days you worked, and your hourly pay, you can quickly estimate both total hours and total earnings with a simple formula.

Work Hours Formula

This calculator uses simple work time and pay formulas.

Total Hours Worked = Hours per Day × Days Worked
Estimated Pay = Total Hours Worked × Hourly Pay

Work Hours Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate total hours worked?

Multiply the number of hours worked per day by the number of days worked.

Can this calculator estimate pay too?

Yes. If you enter your hourly pay, the calculator can estimate total earnings from your work hours.

Can I use decimals for work hours?

Yes. You can enter values like 7.5 or 6.25 hours per day for more accurate estimates.

Is this useful for part-time work?

Yes. It works for part-time, full-time, freelance, and short-term work schedules.

Does this include breaks?

Only if you include break time in your entered hours. For more accurate results, enter actual paid work hours.

Can I use this for weekly planning?

Yes. It is useful for planning weekly schedules, total hours, and estimated earnings.
