How to calculate work hours

To estimate total work hours, multiply the number of hours worked per day by the total number of days worked.

To estimate total pay, multiply total hours by your hourly rate.

Formula

Hours per Day × Days Worked = Total Hours

Total Hours × Hourly Rate = Total Pay
