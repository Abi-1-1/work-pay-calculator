How to calculate overtime pay

Overtime pay is usually calculated by multiplying your hourly rate by an overtime multiplier and then by the number of overtime hours worked.

For example, if you earn $20 per hour, work 10 overtime hours, and your overtime rate is 1.5x, your overtime pay would be $300.

Formula

Hourly Rate × Overtime Multiplier × Overtime Hours = Overtime Pay

Important note

Overtime rules may vary depending on employer policies, state laws, and whether taxes or bonuses are included.
