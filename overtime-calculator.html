What Is Overtime Pay?

Overtime pay is the extra money you earn when you work more than your normal schedule. In many jobs, overtime hours are paid at a higher rate than regular hours, such as 1.5 times your hourly wage or even 2 times your hourly wage.

This calculator helps estimate how much extra income you may earn from overtime. It is useful for checking paycheck expectations, comparing work schedules, and understanding the financial impact of working beyond standard hours.

Overtime Pay Calculator FAQ

What is time-and-a-half?

Time-and-a-half means you earn 1.5 times your regular hourly pay for each overtime hour worked.

How do I calculate overtime pay?

Multiply your regular hourly pay by the overtime multiplier and then multiply that result by the number of overtime hours.

What is double time?

Double time means you earn 2 times your normal hourly rate for overtime hours.

Does this calculator include regular pay?

No. This calculator focuses on overtime earnings only, not your total regular paycheck.

Can I use multipliers other than 1.5x?

Yes. This page includes multiple overtime rate options such as 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, and 3x.

Is overtime required by law?

Overtime rules depend on your location, labor laws, and employment situation. This calculator is for estimating pay only.
