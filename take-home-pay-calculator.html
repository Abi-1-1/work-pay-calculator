How to calculate take-home pay

To estimate take-home pay, subtract your estimated taxes from your gross pay.

This gives a simplified net pay estimate that can help with basic budgeting and pay planning.

Formula

Gross Pay × (1 - Tax Rate ÷ 100) = Take-Home Pay

Important note

This is a simple estimate and does not include exact federal, state, local, or payroll tax rules.

Example

If your gross pay is $3,000 and your estimated tax rate is 20%, your estimated take-home pay is $2,400 after tax.

Who this calculator is for

This tool is useful for workers, freelancers, students, and anyone who wants a simple estimate of what they may keep after taxes.

Important limitations

This is not an exact tax calculator. It does not include real federal, state, local, payroll, or personal tax situations and should be used for rough estimates only.
