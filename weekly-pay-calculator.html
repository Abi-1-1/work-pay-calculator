What Is Weekly Pay?

Weekly pay is the amount of money you earn in one week of work. For hourly workers, weekly pay is usually calculated by multiplying hourly wage by the number of hours worked during the week.

This calculator helps estimate gross weekly income and a simple after-tax weekly pay result. It is useful for part-time work, full-time jobs, side income planning, and paycheck comparisons.

Weekly Pay Formula

This calculator uses simple weekly earnings formulas.

Gross Weekly Pay = Hourly Pay × Hours per Week
Estimated Taxes = Gross Weekly Pay × (Tax Rate ÷ 100)
Net Weekly Pay = Gross Weekly Pay − Estimated Taxes

Weekly Pay Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate weekly pay?

Weekly pay is usually calculated by multiplying hourly pay by hours worked per week. If you want net pay, subtract estimated taxes from gross weekly pay.

How much is 40 hours a week at $20 per hour?

At $20 per hour for 40 hours, gross weekly pay is $800 before tax.

Can I calculate weekly pay after tax?

Yes. This calculator includes a tax rate field so you can estimate net weekly pay after tax.

Is this calculator exact?

No. This is a simplified estimate. Actual paycheck deductions may vary depending on taxes, benefits, state rules, and employer payroll details.

Can I use this for part-time work?

Yes. You can enter any hourly rate and any number of weekly hours for part-time calculations.

Why is weekly pay useful for budgeting?

Weekly pay gives you a shorter-term view of income, which can help with spending, saving, and planning regular expenses.
