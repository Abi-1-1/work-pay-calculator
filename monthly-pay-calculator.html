How to calculate monthly pay

To estimate monthly pay, multiply your hourly rate by weekly hours and then by the average number of weeks in a month.

To estimate monthly pay after tax, apply your estimated tax rate to your monthly gross pay.

Formula

Hourly Rate × Weekly Hours × Weeks per Month = Monthly Pay

Why use this calculator?

This calculator helps estimate monthly earnings for budgeting, job planning, and comparing income options.

Example

If you earn $20 per hour, work 40 hours per week, and use 4.33 weeks per month, your estimated monthly pay is about $3,464 before tax.

Who this calculator is for

This calculator is useful for monthly budgeting, job comparisons, financial planning, and estimating expected work income over a typical month.

Important limitations

This is a simplified estimate. Actual monthly income may differ because of taxes, unpaid time off, variable weekly hours, holidays, and payroll schedules.
