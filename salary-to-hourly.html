What Is Salary to Hourly Conversion?

Salary to hourly conversion helps you estimate how much a fixed annual salary is worth per hour based on your work schedule. This is useful when comparing salaried jobs to hourly jobs, understanding compensation, or planning income more accurately.

If you know your annual salary, the number of hours you work each week, and the number of weeks you work per year, you can estimate your hourly rate with a simple formula. This gives you a clearer view of how much you are earning for each hour of work.

Salary to Hourly Formula

This calculator uses a simple hourly wage formula.

Hourly Pay = Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year)

Salary to Hourly Calculator FAQ

What is salary to hourly conversion?

Salary to hourly conversion is a way to estimate how much an annual salary equals per hour based on your work schedule.

How do I calculate hourly pay from salary?

Divide annual salary by total hours worked in a year. Total hours are usually hours per week multiplied by weeks worked per year.

How much is $50,000 a year per hour?

If you work 40 hours per week for 52 weeks per year, $50,000 per year is about $24.04 per hour.

How much is $60,000 a year hourly?

If you work 40 hours per week for 52 weeks, $60,000 per year is about $28.85 per hour.

Does this calculator include taxes?

No. This calculator estimates gross hourly pay before taxes and deductions.

Can I use this for part-time work?

Yes. You can enter your own weekly hours and weeks worked per year for a part-time estimate.
