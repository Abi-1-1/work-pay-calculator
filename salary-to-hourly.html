How to convert salary to hourly pay

To estimate hourly pay from annual salary, divide your yearly salary by the total number of hours worked in a year.

For example, if your annual salary is $52,000 and you work 40 hours per week for 52 weeks per year, your estimated hourly pay is $25.

Formula

Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year) = Hourly Pay
