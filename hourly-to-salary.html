How to convert hourly pay to salary

To estimate annual salary from hourly pay, multiply your hourly rate by the number of hours worked per week and then by the number of weeks worked per year.

For example, if you earn $20 per hour, work 40 hours per week, and work 52 weeks per year, your estimated annual salary would be $41,600.

Formula

Hourly Rate × Hours per Week × Weeks per Year = Annual Salary
