What Is Hourly to Salary Conversion?

Hourly to salary conversion helps you estimate how much an hourly wage may become over a week, month, or full year based on your work schedule. This is useful when comparing hourly jobs, reviewing offers, or understanding long-term income more clearly.

If you know your hourly pay, how many hours you work per week, and how many weeks you work per year, you can estimate total income with a simple formula. This helps turn small hourly numbers into a bigger picture for financial planning.

Hourly to Salary Formula

This calculator uses simple pay conversion formulas.

Weekly Income = Hourly Pay × Hours per Week
Annual Income = Hourly Pay × Hours per Week × Weeks per Year
Monthly Income = Annual Income ÷ 12

Hourly to Salary Calculator FAQ

How do I convert hourly pay to salary?

Multiply your hourly wage by the number of hours you work per week, then multiply that number by the weeks you work per year.

How much is $20 an hour per year?

If you work 40 hours per week for 52 weeks per year, $20 an hour is $41,600 per year.

How much is $25 an hour annually?

If you work 40 hours per week for 52 weeks, $25 an hour is $52,000 per year.

Does this calculator include overtime?

No. This calculator estimates regular income based on normal weekly hours and does not include overtime pay.

Can I use this for part-time work?

Yes. You can enter any number of hours per week and weeks per year for a part-time estimate.

Is this calculator before tax or after tax?

This calculator estimates gross income before taxes and deductions.
